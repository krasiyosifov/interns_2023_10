Information

Welcome to MathMate! - a game for entertainment and knowledge!

  • To start the game click the "Play" button.

  • You can change the difficulty of the game with the third button in the home scene.

  • On the game scene you have a keypad with numbers and a mathematical problem. The type of the problem can be modified by the buttons with mathematical operations.
    - On the "normal" mode you will have 5 problems to solve.
    - On the "extreme" mode you will have to solve problems until you make a mistake.

  • Answer the problem with the keypad and then press the button "Enter".

  • You can click the "Clear" or "Backspace" buttons to erase your wrong answer.

  • When you answer all problems, you will see your result. Then you can choose to play again, start on the next level of difficulty or end the game.

  • After you end the game you will see the summary statistics of your game.
