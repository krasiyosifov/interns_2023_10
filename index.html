MathMate

Information

Welcome to MathMate! - a game for entertainment and knowledge!

  • To start the game click the "Play" button.

  • On the game scene you have a keypad with numbers and a mathematical problem. The type of the problem can be modified by the buttons with mathematical operations. You will have 5 problems to solve.

  • Answer the problem with the keypad and then press the button "Enter".

  • You can click the "Clear" button to erase your wrong answer.

  • When you answer all problems, the end scene will show to give you the results. Then you can choose to play again or start on the next level of difficulty.
